## Why This Matters

1. **Voice Preservation**: As we age or face health challenges, our voice can change. This project lets you capture and preserve your voice at its best.

2. **Accessibility**: People who lose their voice due to illness can continue to communicate with their own voice, not a generic synthetic one.

3. **Personal AI**: Your AI assistant should sound like you want it to sound‚Äîwhy not make it sound like you?

4. **Creative Expression**: Content creators can generate voiceovers, podcasts, or videos using their own voice without hours of recording.

## Success Metrics

1. **Voice Similarity**: Cloned voice should be indistinguishable from original in blind tests
2. **Latency**: < 500ms for real-time voice conversion
3. **Quality**: MOS (Mean Opinion Score) > 4.0
4. **Usability**: Non-technical users can set up in under 30 minutes
